ABOUT ME

EVGENY IVANCHENKO

Welcome to my website! Photo-creation is a fascinating process that takes up almost all my free time from the main work. The main and favorite genres are outdoor photography, as well as studio art portrait photography, and the genre of nu-art. In addition to the shooting itself, an important point for me is the processing of frames, I follow the work of world-famous photographers, I always study, because new methods and methods of processing allow you to create beautiful unusual pictures, to keep up with the times. But since this is a creative process, and everyone has their own view and their own vision of the world, the work of photographers is different, they, as different artists, cannot paint the same landscape or portrait in the same way.
